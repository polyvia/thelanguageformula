A Project Completed

The Language Formula ran from 2023–2025 — a focused experiment in helping people move toward rapid, confident, independent language learning. The program has now come to a natural close, and we’re no longer enrolling new students or offering active services.

For those who joined us along the way, the full course archive remains available on Vimeo through December 5, 2026. If you have questions about your course access or next steps in your learning journey, please contact us at hello@thelanguageformula.com.

Thank you to everyone who participated, asked good questions, and brought curiosity to the work. This project existed because of you, and it ends with sincere appreciation.

— The Language Formula team
